Tribute to the Twin Towers

Here is some information about the tribute subject.

  • They were officially known as the World Trade Center Twin Towers.

  • Located in Lower Manhattan, New York City, USA.

  • The North Tower was completed in 1970 and the South Tower in 1971.

  • Their heights were 1,368 feet (417 meters) and 1,362 feet (415 meters) respectively.

  • The Towers were destroyed in the terrorist attacks of September 11, 2001.

  • They were part of a complex of seven buildings in the World Trade Center.
